Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I subscribe to the service?

    Visit the "Subscriptions" page on our app and select the plan that suits your needs. Follow the payment steps to activate your subscription.

  • What are the accepted payment methods?

    We accept major credit and debit cards. For more details, visit the "Payment" page.

  • How does the system improve parking efficiency?

    Our AI algorithm directs users to available parking spots efficiently, reducing traffic congestion and saving time.

  • Can I cancel my subscription?

    Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime via the "Subscriptions" page. Note that cancellations may not be refunded for the current billing cycle.

  • Who can I contact for support?

    You can use the chatbox at the bottom-right of the screen or email us at support@smartparking.com for assistance.
